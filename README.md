# 📊 INFINI Technology: Telecommunications Data Analytics Capstone

## 🚀 Project Overview
This repository contains the Data Analytics Final Capstone project developed for the **K-Youth x GIFT Programme**. The project centers on a simulated telecommunications provider, INFINI Technology, analyzing massive transaction datasets to uncover customer behaviors, optimize product portfolios, and drive strategic business growth. 

## 🎯 Business Objectives
* **Customer Segmentation:** Identify distinct customer groups based on demographics, spending patterns, and service usage.
* **Product Categorization:** Evaluate product performance across the portfolio to align with customer demand.
* **Retention & Growth:** Formulate data-driven strategies to reduce churn, improve customer loyalty, and increase cross-selling/upselling opportunities.

## 🧰 Methodology
* **Data Engineering:** Ingested, cleaned, and merged over **1.4 million raw transaction records** into a unified master dataset.
* **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Conducted comprehensive data transformations and applied **Pareto Analysis (80/20 rule)** to pinpoint key revenue-generating products among 150,000+ items.
* **Advanced Analytics:** Utilized the Elbow Method and **K-Means Clustering** to segment a base of over 4,000 unique customer accounts based on behavioral and transactional metrics.
* **Business Intelligence:** Designed and deployed interactive **Power BI Dashboards** (Sales & Customer Segmentation, Product Revenue & Customer Performance) to visualize KPIs and operationalize insights.

## 📈 Key Findings & Impact
* **Revenue Concentration:** The analysis tracked RM931.05M in total revenue. A single high-value customer cohort (Cluster 3) was identified as the dominant driver, contributing **57.68%** of the total revenue.
* **Tiered Customer Value:** Top-tier accounts ("Q4 Very High") accounted for an overwhelming **88.52%** of the revenue share.
* **Product Drivers:** Mobile Plans and Network Infrastructure emerged as the strongest product-level revenue drivers, significantly outperforming other categories.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the data insights, a comprehensive future-state roadmap was proposed to increase Average Revenue Per Account (ARPA) by 15%:
1. **Targeted Retention:** Implement tiered loyalty rewards linked to Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and deploy a proactive churn intervention model triggered by usage drops.
2. **Personalized Marketing:** Execute segment-specific messaging, lifecycle-based triggers, and geo-targeted campaigns based on regional product affinity.
3. **Portfolio Optimization:** Bundle high-affinity services (e.g., broadband + mobile) and redesign high-cost, low-adoption SKUs into accessible entry-level packages.
